Lasagna

Description

A classic recipe of one of the most loved foods in the world. The ultimate comfort food = homemade lasagna. Family and friends gather around the dinner table at any time for this lasagna recipe. A big warm comforting hug within layers of pasta, tomato sauce, white sauce and melted cheese!

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce
  • White Sauce (Bechamel)
  • Lasagna

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Spoon about 1 cup of meat sauce on the base of the baking dish, then covered with lasagna sheets.
  3. Layer with 2 cups of meat sauce, white cup of white sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  4. Pour the remaining meat sauce and white sacue over the last layer of lasagna sheet and top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden and bubbling.
  6. Garnish with parsley and let stand for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving

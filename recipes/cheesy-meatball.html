Cheesy Meatballs

Description

The best meatball recipe starts with the most tender meatballs, browned in a hot skillet to seal in the juices and get crispy, golden edges. Soaking up rustic tomato sauce flavours underneath a golden blanket of melted mozzarella cheese, Cheesy Meatballs is an easy dinner recipe that will be a hit with your family!

Ingredients

  • Meatballs
    • 3 slices white bread crust removed
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 17 oz ground beef
    • 1 onion finely chopped
    • 2 egg
    • 1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil divided
    • 1 large onion finely chopped
    • 4 garlic cloves minced
    • 24 oz bottle of tomato sauce
    • 1/4 cup of water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven grill on medium high heat.
  2. Combine bread and milk in a large bowl. Press the bread down. Set aside.
  3. Add all the meatball ingredients into the bowl with soaked bread and mix well.
  4. Roll about three tablespoons worth of meat mixture into a ball.
  5. Heat one tablespoon of olive oil in a large well-seasoned skillet. Put the meatballs and cook until golden.
  6. Put in the tomato sauce
  7. Cook and cover for 20 minutes
